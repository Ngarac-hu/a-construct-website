What We Do

OUR SERVICES

Insulation

Proper thermal and acoustic insulation to maximize energy efficiency and comfort in every space.

Framing

Structural and non-structural framing for new builds and renovations — walls, ceilings, and partitions.

Drywall Installation

Seamless drywall installation with precision cuts, tight joints, and a smooth foundation for any finish.

Taping

Expert joint taping that prevents cracking and creates the invisible seams needed for a flawless finish.

Mudding / Plaster

Multiple coats of compound applied and smoothed to achieve the perfect wall surface before painting.

Sanding

Fine sanding techniques that eliminate imperfections and create the ultra-smooth surface every quality finish demands.

Interior Painting

Clean lines, even coats, and lasting color — from priming to the final topcoat on every surface.

Flooring / Tile

Caulking

Watertight, professional caulking around edges, joints, and fixtures to protect and finish every installation.

Who We Are

MEET YOUR
CONTRACTOR

My name is Adrian Gichane — the contractor behind A-Construct. With over 5 years of hands-on experience in the construction industry, I've built my reputation one project at a time: doing the work right, treating every home like it's my own, and never cutting corners.

I got into this trade because I genuinely love building things. There's nothing more satisfying than walking into a rough space and leaving it finished, clean, and built to last. From the first stud framed to the final coat of paint rolled on, I'm involved every single step — not just managing the job, but doing it.

Over the years I've taken on everything from full basement finishes and major gut renovations to detail work that simply had to be done properly. I show up on time, communicate clearly, and leave every job site cleaner than I found it. My clients come back because they trust the work — and they refer me because the results speak for themselves.
